MAIN FACT



Elephants are the largest land animals on
Earth and have a unique appearance with their
long trunks and tusks.They are highly intelligent
and social creatures,known for their strong family
bonds and communication skills. Unfortunately, elephants face
many threats including habitat loss, poaching,
and human-wildlife conflict.
